About

Howdy! I’m Aram, a Technical 3D Artist and Creative Problem-Solver dedicated to transforming ideas into polished, immersive experiences.

Experienced in 3D modeling, animation, compositing, and graphic design, I bridge the gap between artistic vision and technical execution. I collaborate with designers and developers to bring projects to life across games, AR/VR, film, and digital platforms.


Background Highlights:

  • MA Visual Communication (University for the Creative Arts, UK)
  • BA 3D Animation & Visual Effects (Mexico) + Certifications in Game Art (Canada) and Programming
  • Work experience in both Mexico and the UK, blending technical precision with cultural storytelling.
  • Fluent in English and Spanish.
