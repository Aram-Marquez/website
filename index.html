About

Hola! I’m Aram Márquez, a Creative Director and Technical 3D Artist. I love bringing stories to life, whether that’s through 3D worlds, animation, interactive experiences, or the spark of an idea that grows into something magical.


I guide the creative side of projects, working closely with collaborators and clients to make sure every idea finds its shape and every concept becomes something to be proud of. I’m happiest when creating together; exploring, experimenting, and transforming chaos into something beautiful and intentional. My goal is always to keep things moving forward with positivity, clarity, and enthusiasm, making sure everyone involved has what they need to shine.


I started my journey in Mexico with a BA in 3D Animation & VFX, and later completed an MA in Visual Communication in the UK, which opened my world to new ways of thinking, storytelling, and collaborating. Since then, I’ve been building cute games, emotional animations, and playful digital worlds that connect with people.


Whether I’m crafting a character, directing a project, or helping a client define the visual identity of their game, I try to mix technique, storytelling, and joy; because I truly believe creativity works best when it feels good.


Let’s create experiences that MOVE! (literally).
